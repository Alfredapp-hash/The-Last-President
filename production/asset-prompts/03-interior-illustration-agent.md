# INTERIOR ILLUSTRATION AGENT
## Book One — eight Art Brief pieces

> Inherit `00-visual-identity-master.md`. These eight follow the Book One Art Brief verbatim. All pencil/charcoal/archival. No faces. No glow.

Art direction rule (from brief): the drawings should make the reader feel that every image was **found, logged, or sealed — not merely illustrated.**

---

## THE EIGHT (placement anchors preserved)

### interior-01-prediction-card.png — PROLOGUE
> Evidence pencil drawing: a scorched prediction card on a brass tray under cold light, edges burned, faint visible field-headings as smudged graphite (no legible text). Archival paper grain, blood-red stamp hint. Found-object framing.

### interior-02-altered-spreadsheet.png — CHAPTER ONE
> Pencil rendering of an altered spreadsheet fragment: ruled cells and column headings suggested as smudged graphite blocks (no legible text), three columns implied. Torn, annotated, evidence-tagged. Cold, archival.

### interior-03-blue-childrens-book.png — CHAPTER ONE
> Small worn cloth-bound children's book, faded gold title (illegible), no faces, no magic glow. Photographed like a logged exhibit on dark archival cloth. Restrained pencil + muted blue tone.

### interior-04-barens-room.png — INTERLUDE
> Evidence-style pencil sketch of a monitored child's bedroom: bed, shelf of books, a chair, hallway light under a closed door, faint camera shadow in a corner. No person, no face visible. Cold surveillance stillness.

### interior-05-the-chair.png — CHAPTER TWELVE
> Full-page pencil drawing of a single empty chair under one overhead light, an evidence tag dangling from the back on a string. Long institutional shadow. Cold, procedural, never supernatural.

### interior-06-confession-records.png — CHAPTER THIRTEEN
> A fractured records page where numbers dissolve into implied names, schools, hospitals, families — rendered as smudged graphite rows (no legible text), torn and reassembled like evidence. Archival, unsettling, cold.

### interior-07-windowless-courtroom.png — CHAPTER TWENTY
> A sealed windowless hearing room rendered as institutional protection, not spectacle: bare table, chairs, sealed door, no gallery, no people. Cold pencil, deep shadow, procedural restraint.

### interior-08-grounded-aircraft.png — CHAPTER TWENTY-FOUR
> A dark private aircraft grounded on a desert runway at night, boarding stairs down, cold lights, no movement, no figures. Pencil + charcoal, archival grain, still and final. No glow, no drama.
