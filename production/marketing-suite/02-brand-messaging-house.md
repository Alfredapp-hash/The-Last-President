# 02 — Brand Messaging House

## Brand core

**Brand:** The Sump Ledger  
**Series:** Baren Sump and The Last President  
**Promise:** Dark political thrillers about power, prophecy, and the future that's already here.

## Pillars

1. **Prophecy as infrastructure**
2. **Institutional accountability**
3. **Refusal as freedom**

## Voice rules

- Precise, sharp, controlled
- Never over-hype or use fantasy cliches
- Keep language morally clear without becoming preachy
- Prefer concrete institutional imagery over abstract buzzwords

## Approved language

- "Not a chosen-one story. A case file."
- "Children are not evidence."
- "Every accusation is advertising."
- "Some days refused to become evidence."

## Avoid language

- "epic destiny"
- "save the world"
- "magical revolution"
- generic "gripping page-turner" cliches without substance

## Book-by-book framing

### Book One — The Last President

- Entry hook: scorched prediction card + impossible naming
- Tone: surveillance, origin machinery, legal dread

### Book Two — Children of Tomorrow

- Entry hook: accountability narrative + Look-Away Ledger
- Tone: courtroom/system language + moral pressure

### Book Three — The Black Path

- Entry hook: refusal and ordinary life as final act
- Tone: compression, restraint, anti-spectacle ending

## CTA matrix

- Awareness: "Read the series brief"
- Consideration: "Read a sample chapter"
- Conversion: "Start with Book One"
- Retention: "Continue the trilogy"

## One-sentence positioning (master)

*Baren Sump and The Last President is a completed literary political-thriller trilogy where systems fail loudly, language fails quietly, and one child refuses to remain available as evidence.*
