# PR + Media Outreach Playbook

## Target media classes

- Genre and book review outlets
- Culture/politics podcasts
- Substack/newsletter curators for speculative fiction
- Literary communities and book clubs

## Pitch angles

1. Completed trilogy with strong voice and thematic integrity
2. Anti-chosen-one framework in modern speculative fiction
3. Institutional language as thriller engine

## Outreach stack

- Primary pitch email
- Follow-up at +5 business days
- Final close-the-loop at +12 business days

## Press package components

- 1-page series sheet
- Author bio
- High-res covers/box set
- Approved quotes
- Sample chapter excerpt

## Interview prep bank

- Why this trilogy now?
- Why "case file" framing over fantasy archetypes?
- The role of language and accountability in the books.

## KPI targets

- Response rate to pitches
- Feature/interview placements
- Referral traffic from placements
- Conversion lift from earned media windows
