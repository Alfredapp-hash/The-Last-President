# Paid Ads Playbook

## Objective ladder

1. Awareness: video/creative reach
2. Consideration: landing page traffic and email signups
3. Conversion: direct retailer click-through and purchase

## Recommended channels

- Meta (IG/FB): visual hooks and quote-card performance
- TikTok: short concept-driven video hooks
- Google Search: intent capture for title/author/genre terms
- Amazon Ads (if available): title and series keyword capture

## Campaign structure

- Campaign 1: Series hook angle
- Campaign 2: Book One entry angle
- Campaign 3: Ethical differentiator angle ("children are not evidence")
- Retargeting: 7/14/30-day windows

## Creative matrix

- 3 hook headlines x 3 visuals x 2 CTAs = 18 variants minimum
- Refresh cadence: every 10-14 days

## Baseline guardrails

- Pause creatives with CTR below account baseline after 1,000+ impressions
- Scale winners by <= 25% budget increase per adjustment
- Keep one control creative active for benchmark continuity

## Metrics

- CPC
- CTR
- CPM
- CPL (if list objective)
- CPA/CPP (if conversion objective)
- Frequency and creative fatigue indicators
