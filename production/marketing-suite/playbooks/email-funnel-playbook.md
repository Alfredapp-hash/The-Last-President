# Email Funnel Playbook

## List structure

- Segment A: New subscribers
- Segment B: Engaged non-buyers
- Segment C: Book One readers
- Segment D: Trilogy completers

## Automation flows

### Flow 1 — Welcome (3 emails)

1. Welcome + series promise + CTA to sample
2. World framing + core quote + Book One CTA
3. Social proof + press kit + purchase CTA

### Flow 2 — Nurture (weekly)

- 1 insight from the ledger (theme/lore)
- 1 excerpt/quote
- 1 action CTA

### Flow 3 — Launch week

- T-3 days announcement
- Launch day main send
- T+2 follow-up with reviews/social proof

## KPI targets

- Open rate >= 35%
- CTR >= 3.5%
- Unsubscribe <= 0.5%
- Conversion-to-purchase trend positive

## Testing plan

- Subject line A/B tests weekly
- CTA button language test every other send
- Excerpt-first vs proof-first email body test
