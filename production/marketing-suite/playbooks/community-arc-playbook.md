# Community + ARC Playbook

## ARC strategy

- Build curated ARC cohort (20-100 readers depending capacity)
- Prioritize reviewers with demonstrated genre fit
- Provide clear review timeline and posting windows

## Reader community framework

- "Ledger Dispatch" weekly updates
- Private ARC discussion thread/channel
- Early-access excerpt drops for engaged readers

## Review acquisition

- Ask windows:
  - Day 7 post-delivery reminder
  - Day 14 gentle follow-up
  - Day 21 close-loop request

## Street-team program (optional)

- Tier 1: share and amplify content
- Tier 2: create UGC reviews
- Tier 3: host micro-reading groups

## Incentive options

- Signed digital artifacts / bonus lore notes
- Acknowledgment page for top launch supporters
- Early reveal access for future series content

## KPIs

- ARC acceptance rate
- Review conversion rate
- Average rating trend
- UGC volume
