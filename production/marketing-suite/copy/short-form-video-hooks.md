# Short-Form Video Hook Bank

Use for Reels/TikTok/Shorts intros (first 1-2 seconds).

## Hook lines

1. "What if prophecy worked like infrastructure?"
2. "This is not a chosen-one story."
3. "A child was named in a scorched prediction card."
4. "Children are not evidence."
5. "Every accusation is advertising."
6. "The black path is not escape. It's refusal."
7. "Three books. One case file. No fantasy shortcuts."
8. "The future did not wait. That was all."
9. "Power bought tomorrow. Then it named a child."
10. "The story ends with ordinary life, not spectacle."

## 15-second structure template

1. Hook line (0-2s)
2. One scene/quote context (2-9s)
3. Positioning line + CTA (9-15s)

## Example close lines

- "Start with Book One at thesumpledger.com."
- "If you want institutional dread with literary voice, this is your series."
- "Read the case file."
