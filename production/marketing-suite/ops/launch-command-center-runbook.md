# Launch Command Center Runbook

## T-24h

- Confirm all retailer pages are live and links resolve
- Confirm email send windows and segment lists
- Confirm scheduled social posts and backup posts
- Confirm paid campaigns in draft/paused ready state

## Launch day timeline

### 09:00 UTC

- Publish primary announcement post
- Send launch email #1
- Enable paid campaigns

### 12:00 UTC

- Monitor analytics + comments
- Respond to community messages
- Boost best-performing creative

### 16:00 UTC

- Publish second-wave post (quote/proof angle)
- Outreach follow-ups to media/reviewers

### 20:00 UTC

- End-of-day report:
  - top channels
  - conversion snapshot
  - blockers and immediate fixes

## Incident playbook

- Broken link -> patch within 30 min + repost corrected link
- Campaign rejection -> fallback ad set launch
- Email issue -> resend to unopens after correction
- Retail metadata mismatch -> hotfix in platform dashboard + note in tracker

## End-of-day checklist

- KPI snapshot captured
- Spend and pacing logged
- Next-day optimization list created
