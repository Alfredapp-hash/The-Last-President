# Retailer Metadata Sheet — Series
## BAREN SUMP AND THE LAST PRESIDENT (Complete Trilogy)

## Series positioning

Dark political thrillers about power, prophecy, and the future that's already here.

This trilogy (~232,000 words, 84 chapters) follows Baren Sump from scorched prediction card to grounded aircraft—from spectacle to protection to refusal. It is literary speculative fiction for readers who want institutional dread, moral architecture, and a child-centered story that never treats its protagonist as a chosen one.
